Research

Grid-Xinanjiang-Haihe (GXH) Distributed Hydrological Model

Primary author and sole developer of the GXH model — a physically-based distributed hydrological model specifically engineered for semi-humid river basins in northern China. The model has been recommended by China's Ministry of Science and Technology and is actively deployed in operational flood control and disaster relief work in North China.

Hydrological Modeling Flood Forecasting Parameter Estimation

📰 Media Coverage

Large-Sample Streamflow Dataset & Pattern Identification

Primary author and sole developer of a large-sample streamflow indices dataset, with 17,000+ views and 31,000+ downloads from 60+ countries. Building on this dataset, identified emerging global patterns in intra-annual streamflow variability.

Big Data Data Analysis Streamflow Regime

Dry/Wet Variability & Attribution

Reconstructed a 547-year dry/wet history of the Weihe River Basin by integrating historical documents, station observations, and reanalysis data. Identified and attributed spatiotemporal patterns of dry/wet conditions across multiple time scales. Recognized as an ESI Highly Cited Paper.

Climate Analysis Drought & Flood Paleoclimate
